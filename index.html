What is "Toranoko!" about?

Amongst the students of Ohashi High School is an oddity: a set of identical triplets, the Takasu sisters.
Takasu Haruko, Mitsuko and Katsuko are the children of Taiga and Ryūji, two high school sweethearts.
After graduation and marriage, an event with odds of one-in-a-million would befall them: the birth of their children.
Now, they've enrolled their daughters into the same school where they'd found each other.
But their conception is not their only peculiarity. It only takes one look to see it; they're freakishly big.
Their unusually tall height and outrageously large busts are enough to attract all eyes towards them.

Until the very first day of high school, the sisters have never kept a single friend, thanks to their
inability to fit in with their peers. Their social ineptitude and their outlandish appearances
leads them to be subject to incessant bullying. Thus, with "fitting in with the class"
out of the question, they're left with only one goal: to survive high school. The problem is that they're
a bunch of idiots, so much so that they've become sworn enemies every teacher that comes their way.
Without grades or friends, the sisters can only wonder what kind of lives they'll be cursed to if
they don't find a way to fit into society.

"Toranoko!" is separated into multiple storylines, each focusing on a different child of Taiga and Ryūji,
and the people they will interact with.
